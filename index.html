About this page

This website is not affiliated with any contra dance organization. I've just thrown it together to have a simple place to point folks for an overall summary of the Portland, Oregon contra dance scene.

For more info on the Portland Country Dance Community, see the PCDC website.

The information on this page only covers contra dances and may be out of date. The PCDC calendar lists all local Contra, Family, and English Country dances.

If you use Facebook, get updates from the Portland Country Dance Community group.

I'm assuming you already know what contra dancing is if you're reading this. If not, check out Wikipedia or this very long answer. Better yet, drop in on a dance. :)
